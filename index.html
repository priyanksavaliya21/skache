MORE THAN A LOGO

Our Mission

Our logo is more than just a play on the word “stash” – it represents the fight against cancer. We thought it would be cool if we could donate a meaningful percentage of our revenues to causes we care about – so we’re doing just that. We’ve taken a huge but unremarkable industry – self-storage – and turned it on its head, giving greater meaning to the dollars you spend.

It turns out a lot of people liked the concept too. We’re now partnered with chanties across the country focused on the fight against cancer.
